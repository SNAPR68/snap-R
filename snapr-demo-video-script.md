| Item | Specification |
|------|---------------|
| **Length** | 90 seconds (max 2 minutes) |

## RECORDING TIPS

### Voice
- Speak clearly and slightly slower than normal conversation
- Sound confident but not salesy
- Slight enthusiasm, not over-the-top
- Pause briefly between sections (makes editing easier)

### Screen Recording
- Move cursor smoothly and deliberately
- Don't rush — you can speed up in editing
- Click buttons cleanly (avoid mis-clicks)
- Let animations complete before moving on

### Editing
- Speed up waiting/loading times (2x or 3x)
- Add subtle zoom when highlighting features
- Keep transitions simple (cuts or quick fades)
- Add timer graphic in corner throughout
